SPONSOR RESEARCH BUDGET POLICY (NSF / NIH / DoD STYLE)

Personnel (Salary) Allowed: - PI/Co-PI effort as approved in proposal -
Graduate, undergraduate, and postdoctoral researchers Not Allowed: - Any
salary not listed in proposal - Admin/clerical salaries without approval
Rule: If sponsor did not approve it → cannot be charged. Violations: -
Adding new staff without approval - Charging admin personnel

Equipment Allowed: - Equipment proposed and justified Not Allowed: - New
equipment not in proposal - General-purpose equipment Violations: -
Drone purchase not planned - Extra laptops without justification

Travel Allowed: - Travel in proposal - Sponsor-required meetings Not
Allowed: - Foreign travel without approval - Non-research travel
Violations: - Vacation travel charged to grant - Bringing non-project
people

Participant Support Costs Allowed: - Stipends, travel, lodging, meals
for participants Not Allowed: - Paying PI salary - Buying equipment
using PSC Violations: - Laptop purchase using PSC - Paying staff from
PSC funds

Subawards Allowed: - Approved formal subcontracts Not Allowed: -
Informal payments - Unapproved subrecipients Violations: - Paying
friend’s company - Creating new partners without approval
