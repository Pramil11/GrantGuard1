FEDERAL POLICY (OMB UNIFORM GUIDANCE)

Personnel (Salary) Allowed: - Salary proportional to percentage of time
spent - Salary must be reasonable and necessary Not Allowed: - Paying
more than normal rate - Charging salary for unrelated work - Admin staff
unless exceptions apply Rule: Charge only the percent of salary equal to
percent of time worked. Violations: - Charging 50% salary for 10%
effort - Paying PI for another project

Equipment Allowed: - Necessary research equipment - Purchases under
proper procurement rules Not Allowed: - Splitting large purchases to
avoid bidding - Equipment for future projects Violations: - Two 6k
orders to avoid 10k rules - Buying non-research equipment

Travel Allowed: - Economy class research travel - Travel needed for
project Not Allowed: - Business/first class - Foreign travel ignoring
Fly America Act Violations: - Charging business class - Flying non-US
airline without exception

Subawards & Procurement Allowed: - Competitive bidding - Documented
sole-source purchases Not Allowed: - Personal relationship vendors -
Paying individuals without contracts Violations: - Hiring cousin’s
company - Missing quotes

Documentation Required: - Receipts, invoices, proof of delivery -
Specific justification - Effort reports Not Allowed: - Missing
documentation - Vague justifications Violations: - No receipt - “Needed
for project” without explanation
