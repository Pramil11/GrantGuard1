UNIVERSITY RESEARCH BUDGET POLICY

Personnel (Salary & Wages) Allowed: - PI, Co-PI, graduate assistants,
undergraduate workers, and technicians who directly work on research
tasks. Not Allowed: - Administrative assistants, secretaries, HR, IT, or
office staff unless 100% dedicated to the project. Rule: Only charge
salaries for people doing actual research work. Violations: - Charging
admin staff - Paying someone for work they did not do

Equipment Allowed: - Research equipment under $5,000 - Equipment above
$5,000 with approval - Repairs/maintenance for research equipment Not
Allowed: - Office furniture - Personal-use electronics - Non-research
equipment Violations: - Buying a TV for the lab - Buying cameras not
related to the project

Travel Allowed: - Travel for conferences, collaboration, or data
collection tied to research - Must follow per-diem rates Not Allowed: -
Upgraded seats - Personal travel Violations: - Charging vacation
flight - Attending non-research events

Materials & Supplies Allowed: - Lab consumables, chemicals, computing
accessories Not Allowed: - Office supplies - Decorations Violations: -
Snacks or decorative items - General office supplies

Other Direct Costs Allowed: - Participant incentives - Publication
fees - Research-related software Not Allowed: - Membership fees unless
required - Services unrelated to research Violations: - Paying honoraria
not budgeted - Buying unrelated subscriptions
